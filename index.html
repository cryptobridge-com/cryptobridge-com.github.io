Why use Crypto Bridge

A cleaner way to bridge crypto

Move between assets and networks without handing funds to a hosted exchange. Crypto Bridge focuses on the bridge job that matters: pick the route, inspect the quote, and approve only when the transaction matches your intent.

Your wallet stays in control

Bridge from a self-custody wallet such as MetaMask. Crypto Bridge does not ask you to deposit into a custodial balance before a swap.

Built for real cross-chain intent

Use the app when you need ETH on one network and stablecoin liquidity on another, or when your next DeFi action starts on a different chain. It fits the open, composable model of decentralized finance.

Costs are visible before approval

Bridge timing and total cost can change with liquidity, gas, and network conditions. Crypto Bridge keeps the live quote in the app so you can review the route before broadcasting the transaction.

How Crypto Bridge works

Bridge in three clear steps

The workflow is built for speed without hiding the details. You choose the asset path, compare the route in the app, then sign from your wallet only after the destination asset is clear.

1

Connect your wallet

Open the dashboard and connect a Web3 wallet. Your keys remain with you, and the app only requests the approvals needed for the selected route.

2

Pick chains and assets

Select the token you want to bridge, the destination asset, and the network where you need funds. ETH and USDC are shown here because they are common bridge inputs and outputs.

3

Review and confirm

Check the live quote, expected path, slippage, gas, and bridge timing in the app. Confirm from your wallet when the details match the move you want to make.

Crypto Bridge FAQ

Answers before you bridge

01

What is Crypto Bridge?

Crypto Bridge is a non-custodial DeFi app for moving crypto between chains and swapping into the asset you need on the destination network.

02

Which assets can I bridge?

The preview highlights ETH and USDC, but the dashboard is where current asset availability is shown. Choose the source token, destination token, and network in the live app.

03

How long does a bridge take?

Bridge time depends on the selected route, source chain, destination chain, liquidity, and network finality. Crypto Bridge shows the current timing estimate in the app before you confirm.

04

What does it cost?

Costs can include network gas, route fees, liquidity spread, and destination-chain execution. Review the live quote and gas details in the app; Ethereum explains the mechanics behind transaction gas.

05

Is Crypto Bridge safe?

No bridge is free of smart contract, liquidity, wallet, or network risk. Crypto Bridge is designed for self-custody and transaction review, so you can inspect the route and sign only from your own wallet.

06

Which chains are supported?

Supported networks can change as liquidity and routing options evolve. Check the current list on the supported chains page and verify the live route in the app.
